San Maurizio d'Opaglio

San Maurizio d'Opaglio is a hidden gem in northern Italy. Being there felt so tranquil. It is situated near Lake Orta and it offers breathtaking views. It was a lovely and peaceful escape from crowded tourist hotspots. San Maurizio d'Opaglio is known for its artisanal craftsmanship and connection to nature as you can see from the photo. The town felt warm and welcoming. We were able to explore the location and indulge in local fresh Italian cuisine.
