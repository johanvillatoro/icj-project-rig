Madrid

Madrid was our last destination before returning home. We weren’t able to do much here since we only had a few hours until our departure, but we still got to experience some of what Madrid has to offer, like the Reina Sofía Museum, which houses art by Picasso. One of my favorite locations was the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, where Real Madrid plays. They have been my childhood soccer team, and one day, I hope to go back and see a Real Madrid home game there.
