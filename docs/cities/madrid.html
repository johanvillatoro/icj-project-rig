Madrid

Madrid is a vibrant city that pulses with energy, art, and life. Spain's capital is a paradise for culture enthusiasts, with world-renowned museums like the Prado and the Reina Sofía housing masterpieces by Goya and Picasso. Its lively neighborhoods, such as Malasaña and La Latina, are filled with tapas bars, trendy boutiques, and bustling plazas. From the grandeur of the Royal Palace to the lush greenery of Retiro Park, Madrid blends history and modernity seamlessly. The city’s late-night spirit, delicious cuisine, and welcoming people make it an unforgettable destination.
