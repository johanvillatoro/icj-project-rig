Rome

Rome was one of the locations where I felt starstruck. It felt like I was stepping into a living museum. It offers a journey through time unlike any other city. From the Colosseum and Roman Forum, where you can see the echoes of ancient civilizations, to the Sistine Chapel in Vatican City, every corner of Rome told a story. One of my favorite moments was walking through the streets because it was a mix of vibrant modern life and centuries-old locations. Tossing a coin into the Trevi Fountain was a must.
