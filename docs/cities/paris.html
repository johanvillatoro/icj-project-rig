Paris

Paris was an incredible vacation destination because it has such a rich history, stunning architecture, and a vibrant cultural scene. Of course, we visited the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre. I love art, and what better place to be than France? I got to see the Mona Lisa painting, the Venus de Milo statue, and various well-known Renaissance artworks. Every corner of the city felt like a postcard. The cuisine was unparalleled—we tried everything from fresh-baked croissants to Michelin-starred dining experiences. We also rode a boat down the Seine River and saw a show at the amazing Moulin Rouge. Why did they let me inside? I don’t know; European age laws are crazy, but Paris delivered unforgettable moments at every turn.
